Module Name: system¶

system.power¶

Utility for turn on, off and check status of power.

Check status:

fab system.power:[hostname],status

Output:

[hostname]
-------------------------------------------------
Chassis Power is [on/off]

Turn on the power:

fab system.power:[hostname],on

Turn off the power:

fab system.power:[hostname],off
